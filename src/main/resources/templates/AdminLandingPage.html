Welcome to VakaPo Admin!

This portal is your primary tool for monitoring and managing the country's vaccination program. It's a centralized, secure platform that gives you access to real-time data, allowing you to track progress and ensure our public health initiatives are efficient. We designed this system to streamline vaccination records and provide you with the essential metrics you need to make informed decisions that protect our nation's health.

Please be advised that this portal contains sensitive public health data, so use it with the utmost care and diligence. To ensure data integrity and security, all actions within this system are logged. If you require any assistance, you can refer to the support documentation or contact our help desk.
